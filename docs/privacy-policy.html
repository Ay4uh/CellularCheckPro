Privacy Policy for Cellular Check Pro

Effective Date: February 7, 2026

Introduction

Cellular Check Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Cellular Check Pro does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the app.

Automatically Collected Information

When you use the app, we may collect:

Device Information:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Android version
  • Screen resolution
  • Hardware specifications (CPU, RAM, storage)
  • Battery status
  • Sensor availability

App Usage Data:

  • Test results (pass/fail status)
  • Features accessed
  • App crashes and errors

Advertising Data:

  • Ad impressions and interactions (via Google AdMob)
  • Advertising ID

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide core app functionality (hardware testing)
  • Display device specifications
  • Generate test reports
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Display relevant advertisements

Data Sharing

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

Google AdMob:

  • Purpose: Display advertisements
  • Data Collected: Advertising ID, device info, ad interactions
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

Permissions

Cellular Check Pro requests the following permissions:

Camera:

  • Purpose: Camera hardware test only
  • When Used: Only when you manually start the camera test
  • Data Storage: Photos are not saved or transmitted

Microphone:

  • Purpose: Microphone hardware test only
  • When Used: Only when you manually start the microphone test
  • Data Storage: Audio recordings are not saved or transmitted

Location:

  • Purpose: GPS functionality test only
  • When Used: Only when you manually start the GPS test
  • Data Storage: Location data is not saved or transmitted

Storage:

  • Purpose: Generate and save test reports
  • When Used: Only when you manually generate a report
  • Data Storage: Reports are saved locally on your device only

All permissions are used only when you manually initiate the corresponding test. No data is collected in the background.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers
  • Test results are stored locally on your device
  • Permissions are only used during active testing

Data Retention

  • Test results are stored locally on your device until you clear app data
  • No data is retained on external servers
  • You can delete all app data at any time through Android settings

Children's Privacy

Cellular Check Pro does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences and does not target children.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access data stored by the app
  • Delete app data through Android settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads through Android settings
  • Uninstall the app at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
